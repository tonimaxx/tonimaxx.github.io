## 🌟 Introduction

Web3 and blockchain technology represent the next evolution of the internet, focusing on decentralization, transparency, and user ownership.

### Why Web3 & Blockchain?
* Decentralized control
* Transparent operations
* Immutable records
* Digital ownership
* Programmable value
* Trust minimization
